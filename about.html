What is Safe Home, and how can we help you?

We are a nonprofit organization founded with the aim to aid those who find themselves without a home or support system to help them.

Here at Safe House, we offer a variety of resources, including – but not limited to – temporary housing assistance, our food pantry and hot meals. We also offer counseling in a wide variety of areas, including mental health, job advice and connection to social services.

OUR SERVICES INCLUDE:

  • Food Pantry
  • Hot Meals
  • Clothing / Toiletries
  • Local Resources
  • Counseling
